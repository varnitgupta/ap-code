Experience the Future of Assessments with your own AI Assistant!

Revolutionize your teaching and learning experiences with AI-assisted assessment authoring and grading.

Assisted Authoring - AI-Generated Questions

Forget spending hours creating questions for your assessments. AI assistant will craft well-structured and engaging questions based on your chosen parameters question type, marks, syllabus topic, and difficulty level. This ensures your assessments are always tailored to your students' needs and cover the required learning objectives.

Assisted Authoring - Learn from Any Source

Assistant can analyze and generate questions from any text or video source, making it easy to create assessments based on diverse content. This helps create a richer and more comprehensive learning experience for your students, exposing them to a variety of materials and perspectives.

Assisted Authoring - A Never-Ending Supply of Questions

Generate questions similar to those already present in our comprehensive question bank library. This means you'll have access to an ever-expanding, virtually limitless collection of customizable questions, each tagged with the appropriate parameters, making it a breeze to select and add them to your assessments.

Auto-Generate Step-by-Step Solutions

Utilize Assistant to effortlessly generate step-by-step solutions for any question. Provide clear, detailed explanations that walk students through each solution step, making it easy for them to grasp critical concepts and methodologies.

Grading Revolutionized

Experience a whole new level of efficiency with our AI-powered grading system. AssessPrep's advanced AI assistant can swiftly and accurately grade student answers, reducing human error and freeing up valuable time for you to focus on nurturing and guiding your students' growth.

AI Detection and Plagiarism Check

Promote authenticity and accountability with AssessPrep's AI detection feature, which accurately identifies whether a response was written by a student or an AI. Additionally, our plagiarism check feature, flags instances of duplicated text by comparing student answers to an extensive database of sources.

Controlled Access to ChatGPT

Promote ethical AI use by enabling controlled access to ChatGPT within AssessPrep. Help students develop critical analysis skills for AI-generated content and encourage them to pose relevant questions to the bot. The system also highlights and appropriately credits copied AI-written text. By limiting access and monitoring usage, you can ensure that students use this powerful tool responsibly and ethically, enhancing their learning experience without compromising the value of their education.
